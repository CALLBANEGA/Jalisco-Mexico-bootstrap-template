About The State Jalisco

Jalisco is a state located in western Mexico, known for its rich culture, stunning natural beauty, and delicious cuisine. The state's capital and largest city is Guadalajara, a vibrant metropolis with a population of over 1.5 million people.

Food And Its Notoriety

Jalisco is also home to a thriving culinary scene. The state's signature dish is birria, a spicy stew made with goat meat or beef. Other popular dishes include pozole, a hearty soup made with hominy and meat, and tortas ahogadas, a type of sandwich drowned in spicy tomato sauce.
